About Us
Ittehad Tahafuz Foundation is exclusively engaged and supports charitable activities. It is purely for charitable purposes, particularly supporting education, health-care and other related relief services. ITF is dedicated to the services of humanity, without any discrimination of creed, religion and political association.

Our Mission
"At Ittihad Tahafuz Foundation, our mission is to safeguard and empower vulnerable communities through comprehensive initiatives aimed at promoting education, healthcare, and socio-economic development. Rooted in compassion and driven by a commitment to social justice, we strive to create a world where every individual, regardless of their circumstances, has access to essential resources and opportunities for a dignified and fulfilling life. Through collaborative partnerships and innovative solutions, we work tirelessly to address the root causes of inequality and injustice, fostering sustainable change and uplifting communities towards a brighter future."
